Looking for some tips? Here's a short list!

* No resources? Hiring a worker under the 'Jobs' tab allows them to take an action slot to collect resources. Be warned though, workers can only occupy one slot!
* Can't level up workers? Try clicking on the item in their worker card to remove items from your inventory tab to pay the cost. Once you've cleared all the items, make sure you have enough gold and press the 'Purchase' Button to level them up!
