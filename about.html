Meet the wonderful people who make this game and site possible!

John Akerson

Development Lead

Akerson went from drunk streamer for an obsolete game, to Dungeon Master of the most popular D&D 5E podcast in Aragonia, and most notably as Head Code Monkey of Abqu Heroes. His biggest contribution to the project, however, was naming the game after Abqu to give him all the credit. No jokes or content after this point have ever been as important.

Richard Pichardo

Front-End Developer

Richard works on the game and site's overall front-end experience and design using hamster magic and powered by a strict diet of freshly grown organic Hobbit-Approved lettuce from the farm lands of The Shire.

Abqu

Game Designer

When he's not annoying Akerson with questions or breaking Richard's wizardry, Abqu works on the game's design. This includes rough balance, the naming of things, and finding creative ways to make tiny mistakes.
